# Combat

In combat the attributes form a triangle:
    - Strength is defended by Agility
    - Agility is defended by Intelligence
    - Intelligence is defended by Strength

On a creatures turn they may take one of these actions:

### Attack
Choose a stat and a creature, you both roll a d6 and add it to your stat
(so if the attacker chooses Strength the defender rolls Agility)
If the attacker scores higher they deal the difference in damage to the defender.
If the defender wins they gain momentum equal to the difference.

### Evade
Until the start of your next turn you roll defence with advantage (roll 2d6 drop the lowest).

### Push
Burn 5 momentum to gain another action. (Because burning takes an action, technically this grants two)

### Escape
Try to run, if you aren't hit before the start of your next turn you escape.
